What We Like

Powerful 3400 PSI Cleaning Performance: Delivers impressive cleaning force that tackles years of built-up grime, oil stains, and stubborn dirt with professional-grade effectiveness

Easy Assembly and Reliable Starting: Arrives fully assembled with CRX 208cc engine that consistently starts within 2-3 pulls, providing frustration-free operation from day one

Maintenance-Free Pump Design: The OEM Technologies axial cam pump requires no oil changes and includes thermal relief to prevent overheating during extended cleaning sessions

What We Don't Like

Hose Quality Issues:

The included 25-foot MorFlex hose is notably stiff and inflexible, with multiple user reports of premature failure and difficulty maneuvering around obstacles.

Proprietary CRX Engine:

While reliable for residential use, the CRX engine lacks the proven long-term commercial track record of Honda engines found in premium models.

Outstanding Performance for the Price Point

The defining characteristic of the Simpson CM61083 Clean Machine is its ability to deliver 3400 PSI of cleaning power at an exceptionally competitive price. This level of performance effectively removes years of accumulated grime, mildew, and even stubborn oil stains from concrete surfaces. Whether you're preparing house siding for painting, restoring a weathered deck, or making your driveway look new again, this machine provides the force needed to complete the job efficiently. The 2.5 GPM flow rate ensures adequate water volume to rinse away loosened debris, creating a balanced cleaning system that significantly reduces the time required compared to lower-powered alternatives.

Engine and Pump: Reliability Where It Counts

The heart of the CM61083 is its CRX 208cc residential engine, which consistently impresses users with its reliable starting capability. Unlike temperamental engines that require multiple attempts, the CRX typically fires up on the first or second pull, providing immediate access to cleaning power. This reliability is paired with an OEM Technologies axial cam pump that eliminates maintenance headaches through its oil-free design. The pump includes thermal relief protection, automatically releasing pressure when temperatures rise during extended use, preventing costly damage from overheating.

User-Friendly Design Built for Real-World Use

Simpson has prioritized practical usability in the CM61083's design. The pressure washer arrives fully assembled, requiring only the addition of engine oil and fuel before first use. The compact steel frame construction provides durability while maintaining a manageable 61-pound weight. The 10-inch premium pneumatic wheels are particularly noteworthy, allowing smooth movement across various terrains from concrete to grass without the frustration of getting stuck or requiring excessive effort to maneuver.

Versatile Accessories for Every Cleaning Task

The included accessories demonstrate thoughtful engineering for diverse cleaning applications. The ergonomic spray gun reduces hand fatigue during extended sessions, while the 16-inch steel wand provides comfortable reach without requiring excessive bending. Four quick-connect nozzles allow instant adaptation to different tasks: the 0° red nozzle for intensive spot cleaning, the 15° yellow for paint stripping, the 40° white for delicate surfaces, and the black soap nozzle for detergent application. This versatility eliminates the need for additional purchases to handle various cleaning scenarios.

  • Dimensions: 37"L x 30"W x 21"H
