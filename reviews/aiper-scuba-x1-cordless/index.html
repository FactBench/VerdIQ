Revolutionary MicroMesh™ Filtration

The AIPER Scuba X1 features the industry's finest filtration system with its MicroMesh™ technology. Capable of capturing particles as small as 3 microns, this ultra-fine filtration removes not just visible debris but also microscopic contaminants that cloud your pool water.

WaveLine™ Waterline Excellence

One of the most challenging areas to clean is the waterline, where oils, sunscreen, and debris accumulate. The X1's WaveLine™ technology specifically targets this problem area with specialized scrubbing action that removes stubborn buildup and prevents unsightly ring formation.

Uncompromised Cordless Power

Despite being cordless, the Scuba X1 delivers an impressive 6,600 GPH suction power that rivals many corded models. This powerful suction, combined with the 180-minute runtime, ensures your pool receives a thorough cleaning without compromising on performance.

Intelligent OmniSense+™ Navigation

The OmniSense+™ navigation system uses advanced sensors to map your pool and adapt to its unique layout. This intelligent system ensures efficient coverage while avoiding obstacles and preventing the cleaner from getting stuck in corners or on steps.
