SwitchFit™ Dual-Height: Two Pillows in One

The CloudAlign pillow's patent-pending SwitchFit™ design offers two loft levels. Use the 3.5" side for back and stomach sleepers and flip to the 4.3" side for side sleepers or extra lift. Quick, tool-free customization adapts to your nightly needs without having to purchase multiple pillows or constantly adjust layers.

Six-Zone Ergonomics: Chiropractor-Developed Support

The CloudAlign pillow features chiropractor-developed six-zone construction. Zone 1: Head Cradle maintains natural cervical curve. Zone 2: Neck Support prevents awkward forward or backward tilt. Zone 3: Shoulder Relief Wings create space for shoulders to nestle comfortably. Zones 4-6: Enhanced support features for mid-back, lower back, and lateral alignment to keep spine neutral all night. This isn't just marketing, it's biomechanical engineering designed to keep your entire upper body in healthy alignment throughout the night.

CloudSoft™ Memory Foam: Comfort That Lasts

CloudAlign uses CloudSoft™ slow-rebound memory foam engineered to outlast cheap alternatives. While bargain pillows flatten within weeks, the CloudAlign's foam returns to its original shape every morning, maintaining loft and support for 3-5 years. This slow-response foam contours precisely to your head and neck with that classic memory foam feel without the quicksand sensation. Deep pressure relief where needed, supportive enough to keep spine aligned. The foam engineering promotes better airflow, reducing heat retention common in memory foam. Even after months of use, the CloudAlign maintains day-one support quality.

The CloudAlign Mellow Pillow is ideal for:

  • Side sleepers who need shoulder pressure relief and proper spinal alignment

If you struggle with morning stiffness, overheat at night, or can't find the right pillow, the CloudAlign adapts to your body and sleep style. The dual-height design, six-zone support, and quality materials deliver the comfort you need at a reasonable price.
