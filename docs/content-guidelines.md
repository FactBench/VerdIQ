# Content Creation Guidelines

## Review Page Structure
1. **Introduction** (2-3 paragraphs)
   - Hook the reader
   - Introduce the product
   - State the review's purpose

2. **Quick Overview**
   - Product specs table
   - Key features list
   - Rating summary

3. **In-Depth Review**
   - Design & Build Quality
   - Performance & Features
   - Ease of Use
   - Value for Money

4. **Pros & Cons**
   - 3-5 pros with explanations
   - 2-4 cons with context

5. **Comparison** (if applicable)
   - Compare to similar products
   - Highlight differentiators

6. **Final Verdict**
   - Summarize findings
   - Clear recommendation
   - Who it's best for

7. **FAQ Section** (optional)
   - Answer common questions
   - Address concerns

## Writing Style
- **Tone**: Informative, honest, helpful
- **Voice**: Second person ("you")
- **Perspective**: First person experience ("we tested")
- **Readability**: 8th-grade reading level
- **Formatting**: Short paragraphs (3-4 sentences max)

## E-E-A-T Principles (Google)
- **Experience**: Share hands-on testing details
- **Expertise**: Demonstrate product knowledge
- **Authoritativeness**: Cite sources, link to manufacturers
- **Trustworthiness**: Honest reviews, clear disclosures

## Affiliate Link Best Practices
- Disclose affiliate relationships clearly
- Provide genuine value, not just product pitches
- Offer multiple options at different price points
- Update links regularly (check for broken links)
- Track performance with wecantrack integration
